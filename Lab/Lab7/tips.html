Travel Tips

  1. Navigation

    Ensure you have cellular connection, or get one immediately, or defer to using a map, as city structure may vary and prove difficult to find your destination, especially if you cannot speak the language, pointing to a destination is very helpful.

  2. Carry a water bottle

    You may be required to walk long distances, and the temperature of the region is also a factor, MAKE SURE TO BRING WATER EVERYWHERE

  3. Always carry a power bank

    Having a dead phone in an unknown environment isn't recommended!

  4. Local Phrases

    Learn a few phrases in the local language, focusing on food, water, hotel, or intended destination
